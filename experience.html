My Experience

From an early age I have always enjoyed working on computers. I remember back in the mid 90s the first time hearing about webpages and thinking they would be just like pages of a book. Little did I know at the time that I would develop a love for the Internet. This love has continued to grow and I would have an opportunity to design and create my own websites.

My first experience of being able to manipulate and format data was in Microsoft Excel by changing to color of a cell depending on what information was showing. The sense of achievement and feeling I got made me a little addicted and wanting to format every cell even if not needed.

After leaving school I went to university as it was perceived that in order to get a good job your needed a degree. At the age of 21 I hade no idea what I wanted to do for a career and picked a degree based on my summer job working in France. When choosing a degree I did consider a computer science degree however I was under the impression that you had to be a genius in order to work with computers. Due to this myth I was feeling discouraged however after watching a few Youtube videos I felt I needed to take the plunge as that burning desire and enjoyment to work with computers was still there.

It has long been known that the half the jobs people do can theoretically be automated. As a result coming from a customer service background I feel it is important to for me to update my skills to ensure I remain relevant in the market place and follow my heart.

Automation and AI will lift productivity and economic growth, but millions of people worldwide may need to switch occupations or upgrade skills. - James Manyika, Mckinsey & Company

Due to all these factors I have now decided to do a Team Treehouse Techdegree. which in the future I believe along with a lot of hard work will support me in the getting the job of my dreams.
